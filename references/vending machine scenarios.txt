| B2 | **A Netflix Subscription** | Funny | *DISPENSED. One full month. Your ship sits outside, fully operational. Since you had opinions about the third season, what better reason than to continue? You write up a strongly worded review. You are not going home... yet.* |
| C3 | **A Battery Manual** | Bad | *DISPENSED. 128 pages. Comprehensive. Covers everything from alkaline, lithium, nickel-cadmium, to even solar cells. Does not cover why humans adore dog videos. Does not explain the emotions and feelings you supposedly had. You read it anyway.* |

---

Ha — "wait, are you?" is the machine having a moment of self-doubt mid-dispense. That's such a funny touch, like the Fate-O-Matic 3000 is questioning its own assumptions in real time. And "Who knows?" as the closing line is perfectly ambiguous — is the dragon a baby? Is that why the villagers keep leaving knights? We will never know.
